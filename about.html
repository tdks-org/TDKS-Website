A home for Kannada language, culture, and community

Tri Delta Kannada Sangha (TDKS) is a nonprofit organization founded to unite Kannadigas and promote the beauty of our language, art, and culture through events, learning, and service.

Our Mission

To bring Kannadigas together to celebrate our heritage, teach our language to the next generation, and serve the broader community through charitable projects.

What we do

  • Cultural events - festivals, performances, and community gatherings
  • Kids & youth - learning opportunities and activities
  • Service - volunteering and charitable initiatives
