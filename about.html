About TDKS

TDKS is an NGO focused on cultural preservation, worship, education and social outreach. We run community events, educational classes, and charitable programs that help disadvantaged families.

Our Mission

To promote cultural heritage, provide spiritual guidance, and serve the community through education and service projects.
