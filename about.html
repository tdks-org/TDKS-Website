About TDKS

TDKS is a volunteer-run non-profit organization dedicated to preserving and promoting Kannada language, culture and community service. We organize cultural programs, language classes, festivals, and charitable activities to support families in need.

Our Mission

To bring Kannadigas together to celebrate our heritage, teach our language to the next generation, and serve the broader community through charitable projects.
